Privacy Policy

At Security Tips Online, protecting your privacy and offering a secure user experience is a priority to us, and we want to make it clear on any data we collect and use on our customers.

The following information will describe the type of personal information we obtain about customers when they visit our Site. This Site is for the general audiences and is not targeted to children, we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. You can contact us at any time if you are unsure, or have questions about our privacy practices.

Personally Identifiable Information

We do not collect any personally identifiable information when you visit our Site. If you follow advertising links to our partners, they may collect personally identifiable information, please ensure you read the privacy policy when visiting affiliated sites.

Non-Personally Identifiable Information

When you visit the Site we will collect non-personally identifiable information in order to provide the best possible user experience for your interests. The following information is collected in your browser using cookies (small files places locally in your internet browser folders) or other internet tracking technology:

  • Your internet domain
  • Your Country, State or City
  • Your internet service provider
  • Your IP address
  • Your Operating System
  • Your Browser Type
  • Your Device Type

This information is then assigned to a unique click ID or Cookie ID in order for our automated algorithms to create a non-personally identifiable profile for each customer.

We may also use 3rd party tracking pixels which will allow affiliated companies to also obtain similar non-personally identifiable information by placing cookie files on your local device.

How This Information Is Used

This non-personally identifiable information is used for us to create and track the activity of our customers and provide a customized user experience that suits interests.

We may also retarget our customers with interest-based adverts on 3rd party advertising platforms that have tracking pixels within our site.

Opting Out Of Non-Personally Identifiable Information

Opting out and preventing us from collecting this non-personally identifiable information may negatively impact your customer journey throughout the Site. If you would like to opt-out, most internet browsers allow customers to disable cookies in the settings, if this is disabled we will not be able to obtain the information listed above. Here is some opt-out information for the most popular browsers:

Firefox: https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies-website-preferences

Chrome: https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/61416?co=GENIE.Platform%3DDesktop

Microsoft Browsers: https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/help/17442/windows-internet-explorer-delete-manage-cookies

Safari: https://support.apple.com/en-gb/guide/safari/manage-cookies-and-website-data-sfri11471/mac
